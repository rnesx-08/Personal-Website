About Me

“WHO I AM”

I'm Ernest Lloyd Flores, a province boy from Misamis Oriental,
Mindanao, who decided to take a chance and come to Luzon.
I'm a shy person who did not enjoy my childhood, but I never
regret doing it because helping my parents has always been my
top priority. Creative crafts are one of my favorite hobbies, and
I recently discovered that I enjoy drawing/sketching but am not
very good at it.
